Silent Tabs Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Silent Tabs is a simple Chrome extension that helps users instantly see and mute tabs that are playing audio.

Information We Collect

We collect absolutely no personal data.

The extension only accesses:

  • Information about your open browser tabs (title, whether audio is playing, muted status) — this is necessary to show the list of noisy tabs and mute them.
  • Your own settings (e.g. whether Auto Mute is enabled) — this is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage.

No data is sent to any server. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising.

How We Use the Information

All tab and audio information is used only inside your browser to display the popup and perform mute actions. Nothing leaves your computer.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. The extension works entirely offline and locally.

Permissions Used

  • tabs & activeTab: Required to detect which tabs are playing sound and to mute/unmute them.
  • storage: Required to remember your Auto Mute preference across browser restarts.

Your Rights

You can uninstall the extension at any time. All local settings will be automatically deleted.
